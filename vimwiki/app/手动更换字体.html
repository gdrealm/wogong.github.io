我 就觉得可以按照类似于kindle 4 的方法直接登陆到kindle上修改字体，usbnetwork成功之后直接同名覆盖相应字体就可以了，这个帖子可以作为参考。 http://www.douban.com/group/topic/23777322/

之前按照mobileread论坛的方法中文总是不成功。 现在直接更换系统字体，中文支持成功。 记录过程如下： 越狱； usbnetwork； ssh或者telnet到设备上同名覆盖系统字体。

那我给你总结一下DXG的汉化： 1. 制作包含中文的字体，或者使用有中英文的字体也行。 2. 把制作好的字体覆盖到kindle /usr/java/lib/fonts/同名字体。 O了。

字体对应关系： No problem, here goes (it may not be completely accurate):

Sans_Regular.ttf: Used in the Settings & Experimental page for the explanation texts. Also for the dictionnary/note popups.

Sans_Bold.ttf: Used in the menus, in the top & bottom panel, and as the collection title when browsing a collection, and the inline text in the book & collection browsers (ie. the 'delete this item' stuff & co)

Sans_Italic.ttf: May be used alongside Sans_Regular or Sans_Bold somewhere ^^ Sans_BoldItalic.ttf: Used in the book list, for the collection names

Serif_*.ttf: Used in the reader, that's the font family your books will be rendered with.

Serif_Bold.ttf: Used in the book & collection browser for the book title & author. (NOTE: For these purposes, Serif_Regular is used instead on FW 3.x)

Mono_*.ttf: AFAICT, nowehere except in the browser, but I may be mistaken. (Might be used in the reader when you're feeding it a raw txt file or some raw html files, depending on styling).

CJK.ttf: Used for Chinese/Japanese/Korean scripts. I18N.ttf: Last fallback for non-latin & non-cjk scripts.

字体替换关系： #Switch fonts in the settings (UI/Reader & Browser)

  1. Caecillia => Serif
  2. Helvetica => Sans_Serif
  3. KindleBlackbox => Mono
  4. code200 => I18N
  5. HeiSeiMaruGoth213_E (J) => CJK
  6. HangulGothMTC_E (K) => CJK
